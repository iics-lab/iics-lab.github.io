Inquiries

Prospective students and collaborators

Prospective students, visiting students, research assistants, postdoctoral researchers, and collaborators are welcome to contact the lab about research related to intelligent interconnect chips and systems.

  • Student applications: please include your CV, transcripts if available, and a brief description of your research interests.
  • Research collaborations: please briefly describe the topic, timeline, and expected contribution.
  • Research areas: SerDes and wireline ICs, RF/mm-wave ICs, low-jitter clocking, AI-assisted IC design, and biomedical mixed-signal systems.
  • Administrative inquiries: please include the relevant deadline, contact person, and requested action.
