Openings

Join IICS Lab

We welcome curious, careful, and hands-on people who want to build chips and learn from silicon.

Who we are looking for

Visiting students, research assistants, master students, Ph.D. students, and postdoctoral researchers are welcome. Backgrounds in integrated circuits, analog/RF design, high-speed links, signal integrity, machine learning, EDA, embedded systems, or measurement automation are all relevant.

Possible projects

High-speed SerDes, PLL/CDR, mm-wave front-ends, AI-assisted circuit optimization, intelligent calibration, silicon validation, test boards, measurement automation, and data-driven design feedback.

Application

How to apply

Please email the PI with a concise subject line and enough material to understand your background and interests.

  • Email subject: [IICS Application] Position - Your Name.
  • Attach a CV or resume, transcripts if available, and a brief statement of research interests.
  • For chip-design applicants, include project summaries, layouts, measurement experience, or tape-out details if you can share them.
  • For AI/EDA applicants, include code examples, papers, project reports, or datasets if available.
  • Send materials to liuzl@gbu.edu.cn or elsie.lll@outlook.com.
