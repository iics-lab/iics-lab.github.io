Principal Investigator

Zilu Liu · 刘孜路

Assistant Professor, School of Information Science and Technology, Great Bay University

Dr. Liu received her Ph.D. from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Her research and industrial experience include high-speed SerDes transmitter front-end design, silicon validation for next-generation communication ICs, AI-assisted automated optimization for T-coil networks, mm-wave phased-array transmitters, low-jitter PLLs, chip-to-antenna interfaces, and biomedical mixed-signal circuits.
